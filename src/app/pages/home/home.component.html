// How It Works

SEND KSHS IN
THREE STEPS

No intermediaries. No waiting for bank hours. Just instant, feeless value transfer.

01
GET AN ADDRESS
Generate a kshs_ address locally — no sign-up, no KYC. Your keys, your money. Use KeyTools or any SDK.
kshs_1mqj8myiphp7uzz...
// 64-char seed → account
02
BROADCAST A BLOCK
Sign a send block locally. Broadcast to the network. Representatives vote. Under 2 seconds to consensus.
process_block({
  type: "send",
  destination: "kshs_..."
})
03
RECEIVED & FINAL
Recipient pockets the block. Zero fees deducted. 100% of value transferred. Check it live on the explorer.
